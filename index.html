About us


Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is an elite international association formed in 1947 to facilitate research and development in the field of IT and Computer Science around the globe.

We feel proud to have been associated with ACM and have established  ACM student chapter in Pune.

JSPM-ACM is a committee focused towards the development and progress of students in JSPM. We work in different fields like Technical, Creative, Publicity, Marketing and Hospitality.

Features

Mission

To provide, nurture and maintain an environment of high academic excellence, research and entrepreneurship for all aspiring students, which will prepare them to face global challenges maintaining high ethical and moral standards.

Vision

To satisfy the aspirations of youth force, who wants to lead nation towards prosperity through techno-economic development

Passion

To provide various technological Learning and to obtain  Technical opportunities.
